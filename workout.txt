^[0-9]{2}: The first two digits represent the state code, and they must be numeric.
[A-Z]{5}: The next five characters represent the PAN (Permanent Account Number) of the business, and they must be uppercase alphabets.
[0-9]{4}: The next four digits represent a unique identification number for the business.
[A-Z]{1}: The thirteenth character represents the entity type, and it must be an uppercase alphabet.
[1-9A-Z]{1}: The fourteenth character is a default 'Z' but can also be any digit from 1 to 9 or an uppercase alphabet.
Z: The fifteenth character is 'Z'.
[0-9A-Z]{1}$: The last character is a check code, and it can be any digit from 0 to 9 or an uppercase alphabet.

ex: 29ABCDE2000A1Z9
